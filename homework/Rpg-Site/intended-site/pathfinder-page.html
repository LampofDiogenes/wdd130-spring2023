Pathfinder

This will contain a hyperlink to DRS20, because much of the work has already been done there already. However, we also offer may modules that you may be interested in here Summary: Pathfinder is an offshoot of DND 3.5, but has been far more embraced by the community. Pathfinder shares a lot of similarities with DND 5e with its fantasy adventure, but boasts nearly double the number of classes, with a staggering amount of prestige classes (which can be understodd as classes with specific requirements). on top of that, the customizability offered with feats is breathtaking.
